Rundown to 1RSA2

Failure to avoid pitfalls

Didn’t do so well for 1RSA1. That’s a fact. Trying to avoid making the same mistakes next block. For now, it’s damage control and cram-time.

Having a system

“Do you have a system?”

I hear that ALOT during clinical day. Says quite a bit about getting things done… Systematically. Reflecting on how I did things in undergrad or even earlier – I’m not a systematic person at all.

Now my bro and I are trying to go paperless and transition to digital media. The principal reasons for doing so are two-fold: Not having to buy paper and ink, and being able to download the damned slides for lecture whenever they are uploaded late/have any last minute changes. (Considering the running cost for printers though, and how much we spent buying the printers makes my heart sink)

So we went through (IMO) a 2, 3-month long hellish quagmire getting crappy Chuwi brand Chinese tablets. Never again. You really get what you pay for. Quality control is not one of them. That wasted so much time and generated so much frustration, it just added on to my overflowing plate. Still wanted to go all digital though. So we got Cube brand Chinese tablets now. Hoping nothing bad crops up along the way now. It’s a gamble with this area of Chinese industry.
